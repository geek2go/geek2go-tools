Tech Notes
Diagnostics Fee: $75 diagnostic fee applies to all repairs and all models. Fee is credited toward the repair cost if the customer proceeds.
Liquid Damage: If customer mentions liquid damage, or signs are found during repair, bump to the ADVREPAIR SKU. Additional costs may apply — contact customer before proceeding.
Advanced Repairs: Current gen (PS5, Xbox Series) advanced repairs may incur additional part costs. Always contact customer before any price increase.
Parts: All repair prices are labor-inclusive. Replacement parts (fan, PSU, HDD/SSD, disc drive, etc.) are billed separately as needed.
Retro Consoles (Xbox 360, PS3, Wii U): Taken case-by-case. No official SKUs. $75 diagnostic fee applies.
